Chosen Topic

I chose "How scams work topic" because it aligns well with my interests and wanted to see if there is more I can learn other than things I already know

Process

It was the first time I have used Socratic style learning and it was pretty fun. I asked AI to explain me as if I don't know anything about the topic and give examples from real life which helped me understand the topic better

What I Learned

Most of the things were not new for me but in this style of learning, I could deeply question things and I realised that scams are actually tied very close to legitimacy of the person offering not the deal itself

Learning Style

I think this learning style has both advantages and disadvantages. It usually works better on the topics you already know and it would be hard to use this style if it is a technical topic

Conclusion

Overall, using Socratic style learning was a good experience and it definitely helped me improve my critical thinking about scams and business.
